### ✅ **Key features of Python**

* **Simple and readable** syntax
* **Interpreted language** (executes line by line)
* **Object-Oriented**
* **Portable** (runs on Windows, Mac, Linux)
* **Large standard library**
* **Huge community support**
* **Used everywhere** — from small scripts to large applications

### ✅ **Why is Python popular?**

* Beginners find it easy to learn.
* Powerful enough for advanced tasks like AI and Machine Learning.
* Has popular libraries like:

  * **NumPy, Pandas** (data science)

  * **TensorFlow, PyTorch** (machine learning)
