COLOR

You can find "no artificial color" ice cream by looking for brands that specifically state this on their packaging, such as the Vadilal Gourmet Natural line or Eclipse Foods. Many local and specialty shops also offer options made with natural ingredients like real fruit pulp, and Aunt Frida provides 100% natural ice cream without artificial colors or preservatives, according to their website.
